How to use Arc Dictionary Extraction


The six easy steps for success!



Step 1: Attach a PDF file

Step 2: Enter a valid page number from where you would like to start your search

Step 3: Enter a valid page number from where you would like to end your search

Step 4: Specify a keyword to narrow your results

Step 5: Enter related text to your keyword

Step 6: Hold tight! - your information is processing for output!
